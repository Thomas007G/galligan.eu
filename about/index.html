About Me

I am a Student

I am currently studying Computer Science at UCC this course gives me great insight into the fundamentals of computing in general, and the history of how the technology has brought us to where we are.

I am a SysAdmin

I have been selected to be a SysAdmin after an interview processs at UCC Netsoc. Netsoc is a UCC society devoted to Computer networking and Computer Gaming. This society is very dedicated to inspiring people from all courses to try to code, I, myself participate at Tech Talks, where we give presentations on certain forms of computer technology.

I am a Mentor

I volunteer at IBM Cork Coderdojo, where I started off, teaching Python to young children. I have since taught many different classes, and am currently teaching basic Web Development. Over the years, I have both taught and learned many valuable lessons with others at this Coderdojo

My Projects

Py The Way

Py The Way is a recommendation system, to suggest which state in the USA best suits the users preferences. I was most involved in planning and design-aspects of this project. It can be found here.

Pressx

Pressx is a modular Content Management System that is currently in development. The aim is to provide the user with an intuitive, eficient backend framework on which to build their website. The backend shall be developed in Google's Go language, which, unlike many CMS's, allows for a highly efficient backend, meaning less cost on server hardware. This project can be found here.
